The Rhythm

How Your Week Looks

Monday

Your program for the week is live in the app — with notes on key focuses and what's changed from the week before.

During the Week

You train to the program. For technically demanding movements, submit a form video. Feedback back within 24 hours — specific and actionable.

Friday / Saturday

Your weekly check-in. How did the key lifts go? Recovery, energy, anything that felt off? This data shapes the week ahead.

Weekend

I review your check-in, make adjustments, and have the next week ready to go Monday.

Your Coach

Who Coaches You

My name is Stephen Wakelam. I've been coaching strength training for over 15 years.

In 2013 I was diagnosed with Peripheral T-Cell Lymphoma — a rare and aggressive form of cancer. During six rounds of chemotherapy and a stem cell transplant, I lost 20 kilograms of muscle. My oncologist told my family I would be "nothing but skin and bone" by the end of treatment.

I decided that wasn't my story.

Working with a world-leading muscle science expert, I trained through every recovery window I had. By my final round of chemotherapy, I had rebuilt every kilogram I'd lost.

I'm not telling you that for sympathy. I'm telling you because it means I know — with more certainty than most coaches ever will — exactly what serious training under serious adversity looks like. I know what works. I know what doesn't. And I know how to get results in conditions most people would use as reasons not to try.

That experience made me a better coach. It also means I have very little patience for excuses — including my own.

Most of my clients stay with me for years. That's not an accident.
